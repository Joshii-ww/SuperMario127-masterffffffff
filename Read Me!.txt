Thank you for downloading the Super Mario 127 0.7.1 demo! 
This game is still in development, so expect bugs.

Default controls:
Z - Jump
X - Spin
C - Use Fludd
Shift - Dive
V - Switch Nozzles
Left/Right - Move
Down - Ground Pound, Crouch, Butt Slide (on slopes)
Up - Ground Pound Cancel
